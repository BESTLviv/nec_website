16-20 May 2018
National University Lviv Polytechnic

National Engineering Competition

The National Stage of the Most Major Engineering Competition in Europe, conducted in two categories: Team Design and Case Study.

The local stage of the competition was held in Lviv, Kiev, Vinnytsya and Zaporizhzhya. The engineering competitions are held annually by the international student organization BEST. Teams - winners of local stages have the opportunity to compete with each other very soon, May 16-20, at the national stage, which will be held in Lviv, organized by BEST Lviv.

Feel the innovation, combine creativity with technology and effective teamwork. During the National Engineering Competition Challenge, we will know who will be able to wear the title of the most engineering team in Ukraine!

Organisers

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) – is a nonprofit organization represented in 95 locations in 33 countries. Since 1989, BEST has provided the exchange of knowledge and cooperation between students, companies and universities across Europe.

Vision: Empovered Diversity

People understand and respect different cultures and societies. An environment where diversity is in power, supports people in the realization of their potential and responsible activities.

Mission: Developing Students

BEST helps students achieve international thinking, better understanding of cultures and peoples, and develop their skills in the international environment.
